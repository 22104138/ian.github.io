Hello! I am Ian Miguel C. Florentino, the creator and designer of this website! I just wanted to say, thank you very much for reading through my website. Originally, the idea of this website was taken from a web development project back in my grade 11 days. It was just a website that I created for fun and to showcase my interests at the time. At the time, me and my friends found it really funny that I named the website "Gwapito" and I guess it stayed as a memory between me and my friends. From time to time they would bring up the website when I would choose to be narcissistic like saying that I'm very handsome, then they'd bring up "Gwapito". Seeing a project that I made just for fun and laughter, now into a fully fledged website made with html and css. It really does bring me joy. It made me realize how far I've come despite all the hurdles that I've faced. It really did make me realize that life will always move on. I am proud. Not just of my website, but with myself as well. Oh yeah, I almost forgot to mention my likes. So mainly I like music, games, and food. The music I typically listen to are metal, rock, indie, shoegaze, electronic, and other songs that just sound good (I sometimes don't have a preference for genres). The games I mainly love are hotline miami 1 and 2 because the gameplay is super fun, Dark Souls 1 and 3 because the lore and worldbuilding is insanely good, and ultrakill because the gameplay is fast-paced and fun. For the food part, I mainly love eggs(although I'm allergic to them) and meat. Yep, that's pretty much it. Thank you so much for reading through a revived project of mine. I do hope this website can see you again.
